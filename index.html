About Suzanne's Place

Located in the heart of Dorrance, Kansas, Suzanne's Place has been serving the community with great food, cold beer, and friendly service. Our bar and grill offers a welcoming atmosphere perfect for families, friends, and anyone looking for a quality meal.

We pride ourselves on our rotating daily specials, selection of beers, and commitment to providing the best dining experience in town. Whether you're stopping by for lunch, dinner, or just a cold one after work, we're here to serve you.
